# Olist E-Commerce SQL Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the **Olist Brazilian E-commerce Dataset** using SQL to uncover key business insights related to revenue performance, customer behavior, product demand, geographic sales distribution, and delivery efficiency.

The objective of this project is to simulate a real-world **e-commerce business analysis** where SQL is used to answer important operational and strategic questions.

The dataset contains transactional data for an online marketplace operating across Brazil, including information about customers, orders, products, payments, and deliveries.

## Data Cleaning

Before performing analysis, several validation and cleaning steps were applied:

* Checked for **NULL values** across key tables
* Verified **primary keys and data integrity**
* Replaced missing `product_category_name` values with **'Unknown'**
* Ensured analysis only used **delivered orders** to represent realized revenue

These steps ensure the analysis reflects accurate business performance.

## Revenue Trends

Monthly revenue analysis shows strong business growth throughout 2017 and 2018.

Key observations:

* Revenue increased steadily throughout 2017
* A significant spike occurred in **November 2017**, likely driven by promotional events such as Black Friday
* Revenue stabilized around **850K – 970K per month** during 2018, indicating marketplace maturity

---

## Customer Behavior Insights

Customer analysis reveals relatively low repeat purchase behavior.

Repeat Customer Rate: **3%**

Revenue Contribution from Repeat Customers: **5.51%**

Insights:

* The majority of customers purchase only once
* Repeat customers contribute a larger share of revenue relative to their size
* Increasing customer retention could significantly improve long-term revenue

---

## Product & Category Performance

Top Product Categories by Revenue:

1. Health & Beauty
2. Watches & Gifts
3. Bed, Bath & Table
4. Sports & Leisure
5. Computer Accessories

Key insights:

* Health & Beauty products generate the highest revenue share
* Watches & Gifts represent a **high-value category with fewer but higher priced items**
* Bed, Bath & Table products show strong demand across both revenue and quantity

The top five categories contribute approximately **40% of total marketplace revenue**.

---

## Geographic Sales Analysis

Revenue distribution is heavily concentrated in Brazil's major economic regions.

Top States by Revenue:

1. São Paulo — 5.06M
2. Rio de Janeiro — 1.75M
3. Minas Gerais — 1.55M
4. Rio Grande do Sul
5. Paraná

São Paulo alone contributes approximately **38% of total marketplace revenue**.

Top Cities by Order Volume:

1. São Paulo
2. Rio de Janeiro
3. Belo Horizonte
4. Brasília
5. Curitiba

These results indicate that **large metropolitan areas dominate e-commerce demand**.

---

## Delivery Performance

Average delivery time across all completed orders is:

**12.5 days**

Delivery time varies depending on geographic location and logistics infrastructure.

Improving delivery speed could enhance customer satisfaction and encourage repeat purchases.

---

## Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, several opportunities for improvement exist:

1. Improve Customer Retention
   With only 3% repeat customers, introducing loyalty programs,
   targeted promotions, and personalized recommendations could
   significantly increase long-term revenue.

2. Focus on High-Performing Categories
   Categories such as Health & Beauty and Bed/Bath/Table show strong
   demand and could be prioritized for marketing campaigns and
   product expansion.

3. Expand in High-Revenue Regions
   São Paulo and other Southeast states represent the largest markets.
   Strengthening logistics and seller networks in these regions could
   further increase marketplace growth.

4. Optimize Delivery Times
   The average delivery time of 12.5 days suggests potential for
   logistics improvements, which could improve customer satisfaction
   and repeat purchase rates.
